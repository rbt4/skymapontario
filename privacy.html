Privacy,
kept simple.

SkyMap Ontario does not require an account and does not operate an advertising profile. The website and app request public weather information needed to display the selected location and forecast.

Location

Your device location is requested only after you tap the location control. A selected location may be stored locally on the device so SkyMap can reopen there. SkyMap does not maintain a user-location server.

Local forecast memory

The Android app may cache forecast data, observations and model-performance information on the device. This supports faster loading, offline context and forecast-change comparisons. Removing the app clears its private application storage.

Public services

Weather requests are sent to public services used by the app, including Environment and Climate Change Canada and model-data providers. Those services may receive ordinary request information such as IP address and browser or app user agent under their own policies.

Ko-fi and external links

Ko-fi, GitHub and official-source links open external websites. SkyMap does not receive payment details from Ko-fi.

Contact

Project questions can be raised through the SkyMap Ontario GitHub repository.
