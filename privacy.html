skymap ontario

Privacy, without the fog.

The plain-language version

SkyMap Ontario does not require an account and does not operate its own advertising or analytics system. Your selected location, map view and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Data requests

To display live information, your browser or Android device connects directly to public-data and mapping providers, including Environment and Climate Change Canada, Natural Resources Canada, Ontario 511, CARTO and OpenStreetMap-related services. Those providers can receive normal technical request information such as your IP address and browser or app user agent.

Location

Location access is optional. When you choose “Use my location,” the device provides coordinates to SkyMap Ontario so the map can centre locally and select nearby readings. SkyMap Ontario does not send those coordinates to a SkyMap Ontario-owned server or account system.

Local storage

The app stores preferences, the selected view and limited cached data on your device to improve startup and resilience. Clearing the app or browser storage removes it.

GitHub Pages and Ko-fi

The public website is hosted by GitHub Pages. GitHub may log visitor IP addresses for security. The support button opens Ko-fi, which has its own privacy and payment practices. SkyMap Ontario never handles payment-card details.

Emergency use

SkyMap Ontario is a visual information tool, not an emergency-warning system. Always follow official alerts and instructions from authorities.
